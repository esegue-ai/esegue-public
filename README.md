# eSEGuE

**Systematic Market Intelligence for disciplined investment decisions.**

eSEGuE is a quantitative research and decision-support framework designed to transform market data into structured, explainable and risk-aware insights.

The project combines equity opportunity ranking, cross-asset regime analysis, market rotation detection, systematic validation and controlled execution workflows.

## What eSEGuE Does

eSEGuE is organized around two complementary intelligence engines:

### Equity Intelligence

Analyzes a broad equity universe to:

- identify and rank market opportunities;
- combine price action, trend, volatility, fundamentals and contextual data;
- distinguish between different signal families;
- filter opportunities according to market conditions;
- support disciplined and traceable investment decisions.

### Regime & Rotation Intelligence

Monitors multiple asset classes and geographic regions to:

- classify the prevailing market regime;
- measure risk appetite and inflation dynamics;
- detect rotations between equities, bonds, commodities, currencies and credit;
- compare regional and style leadership;
- identify changes in market direction and momentum.

## Beyond Signal Generation

eSEGuE includes a broader operating framework for:

- automated data collection and validation;
- systematic signal logging;
- configurable risk and position sizing controls;
- broker-integrated execution workflows;
- portfolio and trade monitoring;
- periodic performance reporting;
- historical backtesting and diagnostics;
- Telegram notifications and operational alerts.

## Technology

The platform is developed primarily in Python and integrates:

- market and fundamental data providers;
- Interactive Brokers infrastructure;
- statistical and quantitative analysis libraries;
- local caching and resilient fallback mechanisms;
- automated scheduling and monitoring;
- GitHub-based version control;
- protected credential management;
- Cloudflare-based web infrastructure.

## Design Principles

eSEGuE is built around five principles:

1. **Explainability**  
   Every signal should be traceable to observable market evidence.

2. **Risk awareness**  
   Opportunity identification must remain subordinate to capital protection.

3. **Context sensitivity**  
   The same signal may have different implications under different regimes.

4. **Empirical validation**  
   Decisions and priorities should evolve through measurable historical evidence.

5. **Operational discipline**  
   Research, execution and reporting should form one controlled process.
